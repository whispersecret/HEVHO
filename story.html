Our start-up aims to create a platform which bridges the gap between people who want to volunteer and the organisations that need them. This project started as part of Microsoft's Imagine Cup but our team was formed long before then. We met at the Centre for Innovation and Enterpenureship's Summer Lab and have since learnt a lot about start-ups and how they function. By participating in the Imagine Cup we hope to further our knowledge and apply what we've learnt.
